About Saheli Connect

Saheli Connect is dedicated to empowering working women by connecting them with trusted, verified house help providers. We understand the challenges of balancing work and home, and we're here to make finding reliable help easier, safer, and more efficient.

Our platform is built on trust, transparency, and community support, ensuring that both employers and helpers have a positive experience.
